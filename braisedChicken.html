Braised Chicken

  • Chicken
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Lemons
  • Sage
  • Thyme (or herds of choice)
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Olive Oil
  • Chicken Broth or Reserved Bean cooking liquid (Recipie Here)

Salt and pepper chicken thighs, wings, and drumsticks (bone in chicken stayed better for braising) add garlic and olive oil to an oven safe pan until garlic is fragrant. Add chicken, skin side down. While browning skin? Chop carrots, onions, lemons, and a whole head of garlic in half. Once browned, flip chicken, add vegetables and liquid of choice, season with salt & pepper. Add herbs and place in the oven at 325F for about 40 minutes or until chicken is cooked through. Let cool for about 15 minutes and serve! Delicious with potatoes or over rice.
