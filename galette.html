Galette

For the Dough

  • 1.5 cups Unbleached All Purpose Flour
  • Healthy pinch of Salt
  • 1 stick unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cups cold water

Combine flour and salt in a medium bowl. Chop cold butter into about 1/4 inch squares. Combine your butter and flour mixture quickly with your hands, move quick to not warm the butter. Once craggily dough is formed, gently stir in water slowly until dough is hydrated evenly but not wet. Wrap up and chill for about 45 minutes in the fridge. Once chilled, you can remove from fridge and on a lightly floured surface, roll your dough into a ~15 inch circle and then fill dough keeping a ~2 inch edge for wrapping around the sides. Place vegetables (for best results, toss vegetables in olive oil and allow ~15 minutes of baking before adding to the galette) of choice and food the ends toward the center. Bake at 425F for 25 minutes. Let cool and enjoy!

For the Dough

  • 1/3 cup full fat Greek Yogurt
  • 1/2 cup shredded Mozzarella
  • Handful Grated Parmesean
  • 1 Egg
  • Lemon Zest
  • Salt, Pepper, Rosemary, and Sage

In a blender combine rosemary, sage, salt, and pepper. Add all ingredients to a bowl and fold together until homogeneous.

Personally I used red beets, golden beets, Brussel sprouts, and squash. I cut all of the vegetables in thin bite sized pieces. I tossed them in olive oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Then baked at 425F for 15 minutes. Pulled them out, let them cool, and then places them in my galette. It worked out perfectly!
